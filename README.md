# ERAYA Planetary Intelligence Ecosystem

The ERAYA Intelligence Ecosystem is a global initiative focused on building integrated intelligence systems across three fundamental domains:

Human Health  
Earth Systems  
Planetary Protection  

This ecosystem connects advanced technologies, environmental intelligence, and sustainable systems to support the long-term wellbeing of humanity and the planet.

---

## Ecosystem Platforms

### ERAYA
Human Health Intelligence Platform

ERAYA focuses on personalized health intelligence, preventive healthcare systems, and integrative wellness insights combining traditional knowledge systems and modern data science.

---

### Bhumisetu
Land & Environmental Intelligence Platform

Bhumisetu develops intelligence systems for land use, environmental monitoring, ecological balance, and sustainable infrastructure planning.

---

### BhumiRaksha
Planetary Protection Platform

BhumiRaksha focuses on waste intelligence, circular resource systems, climate impact reduction, and planetary sustainability frameworks.

---

## Mission

To build an integrated intelligence ecosystem that connects human health, environmental systems, and planetary protection for a sustainable future.
